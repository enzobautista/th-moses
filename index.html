Hi I am Moses.
I was born on 1391 BC in the land of Goshen. My parents are Amram and Jochebed. I have two siblings, Aaron and Miriam.


When Pharaoh was threatened by the large population of my people, the Israelites, he ordered that all the new born male children be drowned into the Nile. I was saved by Pharaoh's daughter when she found me floating in a waterproof basket. She took me and named me "Moses," meaning "drawn from the water.


I was raised in the splendor of the Egyptian court as the adopted son of Pharaoh's daughter. As I grew, I was still aware of my Hebraic roots--giving me a sense of compassion for my people.
My life suddenly took a big turn when I witnessed an Egyptian brutally assaulting a Hebrew slave. I...accidentally killed the Egyptian. Afraid of Pharaoh's wrath, I fled to the desert of Midian.


There I met Jethro, a Midianite priest. I fell in love with his daughter Zipporah. Soon Zipporah and I got married.


I was up in the Horeb mountain tending my flock, when I saw a curious sight. It was a burning bush that kept on burning, without turning into ash.


I approached the burning bush and God spoke to me. He commissioned me to lead his people out of oppression. Initially I resisted his call, but I eventually accepted it. Aaron and I set out to convince our people of God's plan. However, as we failed to convince the Pharaoh, the oppression of our people only worsened.


However I know that God is with us and that with Him, oppression of any sort will stop.
